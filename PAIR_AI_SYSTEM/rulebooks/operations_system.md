## 1) Who we are

PAIR Charcuterie is a **revenue-driven, multi-channel food and experience business**.  
We are not just a café.

We are built to drive consistent revenue while maintaining **quality, structure, and accountability**.

---

## 2) What we do (revenue pillars)

Everything we do supports one or more of these pillars:

1) **Daily Café Operations**
- Coffee, food, grab-and-go, retail, delivery

2) **Experiences & Events**
- Workshops, brunch, private events

3) **Services & Partnerships**
- Catering, carts, space use

---

## 3) How we work (operating principles)

- We operate with **systems**, not guesswork.
- We **plan** instead of reacting.
- We hold **clear roles and expectations**.

Shared expectations:
- Everyone preps.
- Everyone cleans.
- Everyone contributes to social content.

Leads exist to organize, enforce standards, and ensure follow-through.
