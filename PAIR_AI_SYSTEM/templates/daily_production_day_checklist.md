## Daily Production Day Checklist (PAIR)

**Rule**: Every other day is a production day. Saturday is always a production day.

---

## 1) Plan (before production starts)

- [ ] Confirm today is a production day
- [ ] Confirm what must be produced (prep list)
- [ ] Check inventory vs prep list
- [ ] Assign owners (who does what)

---

## 2) Execute (production)

- [ ] Prep completed
- [ ] Items labeled + dated
- [ ] Storage organized (BOH ready)

---

## 3) Readiness (for service)

- [ ] Grab & go / cooler stocked (FOH)
- [ ] Any sell-outs or shortages communicated to GM (Ally)
- [ ] Space reset (FOH + BOH clean)

---

## 4) Closeout

- [ ] What’s ready confirmed
- [ ] What’s missing listed
- [ ] Issues escalated early (same day)
