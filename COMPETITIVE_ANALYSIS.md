## ðŸš€ Phase 2: BYO Charcuterie Bar

### Inspired by Your Staff's Research

**Concept:** NYC's Char-cute-rie model adapted for Pittsburgh

**How It Works:**
1. Customer reserves 30-min timeslot online
2. Arrives at shop (201 Smithfield St)
3. Chooses board size (small $30, medium $50, large $75)
4. Selects from your curated items:
   - 3-5 meats (your staff's list: salami, prosciutto, etc.)
   - 3-5 cheeses (gouda, cheddar, goat cheese, etc.)
   - Fruits, crackers, spreads, garnishes
5. Staff helps arrange on board
6. Takes photos (Instagram moment!)
7. Takes board home or eats in cafe

**Revenue Model:**
- $30-75 per person/group
- 30-min sessions = 10 per day = $300-750/day
- Evenings + weekends = $6,000-15,000/month additional
